Who I am

I'm a Civil Engineer (almost) that started to learn CFD in 2013, while studying as an exchange student at The University of Queensland. In that same year, I heard about OpenFOAM and started to use it. The first experience with OpenFOAM was quite overwhelming, but I pushed through and kept using it, while slowly learning from my mistakes and getting help at the great CFD-Online forum. Ended up doing my undergraduate thesis on the use of CFD to simulate flow past a cylinder using OpenFOAM. Now, I'm setting up this website to share some of the knowledge that I gathered during my (still brief) years of CFD modelling.

Davi Sales Barreira Civil Engineering (almost)

About

My name is Davi Barreira, I am an engineering student interested in the Computational Fluid Dynamics. This website is to help out people interested in the subject and to showcase some of my work.

The webiste focus is on CFD with the use of Python and some Open Source software, such as OpenFOAM, Blender and Paraview.
