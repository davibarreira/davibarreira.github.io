Computacional Fluid Dynamics

Computational Fluid Dynamics, or the more commonly used, CFD, is about using computational simulations to analyze systems involving fluid flow, heat exchange and chemical reactions.

--- Versteeg e Malalasekera, An Introduction to Computational Fluid Dynamics (2007)

OpenFOAM

OpenFOAM is an open source software for computational fluid dynamics, developed by OpenCFD and the OpenFOAM Foundation. It has an enthusiastic and large community and can be used for a broad range of applications. In a few words, OpenFOAM is just awesome.

About
